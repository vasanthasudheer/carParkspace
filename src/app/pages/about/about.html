What this portal is about

No-brokerage parking marketplace for city renters

ParkSpace solves a practical city problem: rented flats without parking. Owners list unused spaces and customers discover & book by area.

Core MVP capabilities

  • Role-based users
  • Vehicle profile with size
  • Space listings with pricing
  • Plan-based visibility
  • Booking lifecycle

Who uses it

Customer: Searches parking, adds vehicle, buys plan, books space.

Owner: Posts spaces, uploads images, manages bookings.
